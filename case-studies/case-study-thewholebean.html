The Whole Bean

Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign

“The Whole Bean” was a complex project for a fictional client - Karla Kahvi, owner of a ready-to-be-open coffee shop named The Whole Bean.

My job was to design branding for Karla’s business and create number of the identity design deliverables. In the first phase of the project I was working on finding right color scheme and a typescale, collecting brand images, creating a logo, an icon pair, and a social media header. At the end of this phase I included all mentioned deliverables in a brand style guide.

Second phase of the project was focused on preparing and organising successful brand launch for Karla. My main goal was to attract customers to visit The Whole Bean during the opening weekend, however, it was also important to start building a long-term relationship with potential customers. During this phase I was working mostly on designing a landing page that would inform about grand opening, show why it’s worth to come and encourage to sign up for a newsletter.
