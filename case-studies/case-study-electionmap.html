U.S. Election Map

HTML, CSS, JavaScript

Project “U.S. Election Map” is an interactive map of United States made with JavaScript. It’s purpose is to show the results of the fictional presidential election.

Names of two candidates, total number of their votes and name of the winner are visible on the top of the map. To check who won in a particular state you need to mouse over a chosen state. Color of a state will change to the color of the winner and more precise information will be displayed in the in the table on the bottom right.
