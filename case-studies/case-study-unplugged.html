Unplugged

HTML, CSS, Responsive Web Development

Project “Unplugged” was part of the “Responsive Web Development” course I enrolled to through Skillcrush.

My fictional client - ”Unplugged” - was a website publishing the latest news from the world of indie rock. As nowadays more and more people visits websites on their mobile devices, Unplugged team wanted their site to be fully responsive. My job was to translate already existing design into a website that looks good not only on a desktop, but also on a tablet and a smartphone.

Website contains three main areas of content: “News”, with information about the latest and upcoming events, “About Us” that includes contact details, mission statement and short description of all team members, and “FAQs” with the most common questions asked by readers.
