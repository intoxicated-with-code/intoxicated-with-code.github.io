Accelerate

WordPress, PHP, HTML, CSS

“Accelerate” was my second project during Skillcrush’s WordPress course.

Fictional client Donna, the chief marketing at Accelerate Marketing, needed a new web developer. Their website has been already created but it was way too simple for Accelerate’s business needs as it contained only a homepage and blog.

Every morning Donna was sending me an “email” with new functionalities Accelerate would like to add to their website. The aim was to work in three, week-long sprints at the end of which my role was to launch the changes I made to the site over the course of the week.

Main changes and functionalities added to the website:

  • Homepage: 
    • - change of the background picture, 
    • - change and update of the navigation bar,
    • - change of the button background color,
    • - new sections: “Featured Work”, “Blog”, “Twitter”, subscription form
  • Social media navigation added to the footer

  • New pages: “About”, “Work”, single case study, “Contact”
