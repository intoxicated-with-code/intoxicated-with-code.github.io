LOLCat Clock

HTML, CSS, JavaScript

“LOLCat Clock” was a fun project within “Introduction to JavaScript” course on Skillcrush.

The website shows different pictures and text depending on the time when user visits it. Wake up time, lunch time and nap time can be customised and party time takes place, well, whenever you wish. Just click the “PARTY TIME!” button and see what happens…
