About Me

Home / About

Hi there!

I’m Paulina and most days you can find me supporting other entrepreneurs in their daily tasks and growing their businesses. I’m mostly known for my organisational skills but I heard that I’m also quite tech-savvy. Some people say that my creativity is not too shabby either!

I love serving the world by making others greater than they are now. I truly enjoy observing my clients getting better results, inspiring more people and becoming happier with their business and life.

I work with people who need help with:

  • email and newsletter design and automation
  • managing social media activity
  • designing graphics for social media channels
  • blog management and administration
  • PowerPoint and Keynote presentations
  • landing page design
  • managing WordPress site
  • SEO of the website or blog
  • transcription of podcasts and other audio recordings
  • calendar management
  • and more

Personal Life

When I’m not busy serving my clients you can catch me baking, reading or practising yoga. I also make sure that our little birdy called Paco always has a full tummy. Just don’t be fooled by the name - Paco is a girl and the one trait we both share is our love for good food. Recently I've been also rediscovering my childhood passion which is drawing and painting. I might not be the best at it but that doesn’t matter - it brings me way too much joy to stop doing it again.


If you’re dying to hear more, here are three things you might not know about me:

  1. I come from Poland but haven’t been living in my country for 3 consecutive years. Also, I’ve had two more episodes of living abroad before my permanent move. Countries I considered home for at least a while are: United Kingdom, Croatia, Belgium, Luxembourg and now the Netherlands. And Poland, of course.
  2. I write a blog in Polish. It’s mostly about my experiences of living in the Netherlands, about my travels, productivity and general topics regarding mindful living. If you wish you can check it out here.
  3. I have a bachelor degree in political science and a master degree in economics. I especially enjoyed studying economics and spent 5 months doing so in Croatia where I’ve met some amazing friends. The best thing is that after all these years, we still keep in touch!
