About

Hi, it’s very nice to meet you!

I’m Paulina, self-taught visual designer and web developer, currently based in the Netherlands. My mission is to help small businesses grow and leave their mark.

Imagination is definitely my favourite tool, but I like to use my other skills as well. As a designer, I enjoy working with Photoshop, InDesign and Illustrator, but my developer nature loves writing code in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and sometimes PHP. One of my professional passions is UX design so I’m constantly learning how to improve my designs and make sure that using them is nothing but pure pleasure.

When I’m not busy with designing and building websites you can find me reading, cooking or practicing yoga. So far I’ve been living in 5 and a half countries and I’m planning to add at least a few to this list. “Gilmore Girls” fan forever. Organisation and planning freak. ASMR addict. Podcasts lover.
